Legacy Fund
Make a gift or pledge to the San Carlos Charter Legacy Fund before the 2026–2027 school year begins.
  • The San Carlos Charter Legacy Fund is a long-term investment in the future of the school.
  • The fund is designed to preserve and strengthen the founding mission and vision that have guided San Carlos Charter for more than 30 years.
  • It honors the values, educational philosophy, and community-centered approach that shaped the school's beginnings.
  • The current campaign is being launched in memory of Stacy Emory, a founder and former Executive Director of SCC, whose vision and leadership helped shape the school's enduring mission and community.
  • At launch, the Legacy Fund is a one-time opportunity for the entire SCC community — including current families, alumni families, former staff, founders, and longtime supporters — to contribute in Stacy's memory.
  • Over time, the Legacy Fund will become an alumni-focused giving vehicle, creating a lasting way for those shaped by SCC to help carry its mission forward.
  • Gifts and pledges will be invested, not spent right away, to create a stable new income stream and support future strategic priorities.
  • This is not a replacement for SCEF. SCEF remains the primary annual giving priority for current-year programs and staffing needs.
  • For current families who have already made their annual SCEF donation, the Legacy Fund is a one-time opportunity to go above and beyond.
  • Donors may contribute a variety of assets, including cash, cryptocurrency, appreciated stock, or other eligible assets.

Other ways to strengthen the school
Daily Attendance
Help your student attend school every day they are healthy and able to be there.
  • Daily attendance matters for student learning, connection, and school funding.
  • When students attend consistently, they benefit academically, socially, and emotionally.
  • Attendance also affects public funding, which helps support the school's operations.
Field Trip Support
Contribute toward field trip costs.
  • Field trips are an important part of the SCC experience and help bring learning to life.
  • Contributions toward field trip costs help make these experiences possible for all learners.
  • Support in this area helps reduce barriers and ensures that cost does not limit participation.

Educare
Consider Educare when your family needs before-school or after-school care.
  • Educare is a valuable program for families and a primary source of revenue for the school.
  • Participation in Educare helps support SCC while also providing care and enrichment for students.
  • Using Educare is one way families can meet their own needs while also strengthening the school financially.
